Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery times?

Standard delivery within Kenya takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for an additional fee with 1-2 business day delivery.

Do you offer custom orders?

Yes! We specialize in custom wooden kitchenware. Contact us with your requirements for a personalized quote.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for unused items in original condition. Custom orders are non-returnable unless damaged during shipping.

How do I care for wooden utensils?

Hand wash only with mild soap, dry immediately, and apply food-grade mineral oil monthly to maintain the wood.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping times vary by location and typically take 7-14 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and PayPal for international orders.
